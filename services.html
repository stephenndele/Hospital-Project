What we offer


Dermatology Services

The Dermatology service at The Kauu Hospital provides both outpatient and inpatient services for the diagnosis and management of dermatological conditions affecting people of all ages. The team of dermatologists at hospital have the experience and expertise to treat almost any skin conditions and will decide on the best course of treatment for patients. They are equally adept at performing a wide range of surgical and aesthetic dermatological procedures. A unique strength of the outpatient service is the close collaborative practice among both dermatologists and plastic and cosmetic surgeons at The Karen Hospital, providing a one-stop service for all skin-related conditions.

Obstetrics and Gynaecology

The Kaluu Hospital Obstetrics and gynaecology clinic comprises of highly qualified and talented specialist consultants, doctors and supported by a team of dedicated O&G nurses and midwives. The O&G Clinic at TKH offers a full range of O&G specialised services including normal pregnancy care, postnatal pregnancy care, women’s health screening, women’s cancer screening, management of common gynaecological problems and menopause-related issues.

Rheumatology Services

The Rheumatology service at The Kaluu Hospital provides both outpatient and inpatient care to patients with autoimmune and metabolic bone diseases. We aim to help patients achieving remission and improve their quality of life. Our mission is to achieve the best clinical outcomes by delivering holistic patient care. We actively encourage patients’ empowerment in their own care and treatment. To ensure comprehensive care for the multifaceted illnesses we treat, we foster a collegial environment and work closely with our colleagues from allied health professions, medical and surgical specialities.



Surgery

The Kaluu Hospital surgeons are highly qualified and experienced with exceptional knowledge and skills to carry out the procedures needed to investigate diseases, repair or remove damaged tissues, improve the function or appearance of an organ or body part. Our operating theatres are equipped with first class surgical equipment and highly functional machines of the highest quality, which surgeons and other physicians use to perform lifesaving procedures.

Otorhinolaryngology

Problems relating to the ear, nose and throat warrant a high level of precision in surgical care. The Kaluu Hospital has the state-of-the-art 3D High end Vario 700 operating microscope that allows for minimally invasive surgeries that are safer and allow patients to recover faster. Our ENT specialists use years of expertise along with high-tech equipment to diagnose a nd treat both common and individual.

Orthopaedic Services

Surgical Care at The Kaluu Hospital spans the entire spectrum of Orthopaedic and spinal disorders including joint replacements, arthroscopy, spinal deformities, degenerative diseases, tumours and spine infections. Our surgeons use advanced procedures to treat spinal conditions. They also emphasize on non-invasive treatments. The Orthopaedic department encompasses the latest high definition arthroscopic systems, joint replacement instrumentation and an orthopaedic operating table.
